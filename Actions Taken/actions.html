What We’ve Done So Far

This campaign didn’t just live in our heads—it lived in our actions. As part of the RedSafe movement, we’ve created a fully functional website that features a variety of tools, information, and personal experiences related to teen drug and alcohol abuse in Marin.

We produced a podcast series, Redwood Recovery: Stories That Matter, where real voices shared their journeys, including interviews with recovering addicts, student reflections, and in-depth discussions about mental health and recovery culture.

We distributed surveys through our homepage form to gather real-time student feedback and perceptions. These anonymous submissions helped us understand just how common substance exposure really is—and helped shape our message to meet the needs of our community.

We also took initiative outside the web. Our team has prepared printed awareness materials, worked with teachers on classroom discussions, and even began planning on-campus events and fundraisers designed to support local addiction recovery resources.

Every action we took was deliberate. Every step we made was built around three pillars: awareness, prevention, and support. This isn’t just a grade for us—it’s our community. And we’re proud to do our part in protecting it.
