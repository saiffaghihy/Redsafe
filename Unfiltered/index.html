Redwood Recovery: Stories That Matter

This podcast series was created by students, for students. Titled Redwood Recovery, each episode of this show features raw, honest conversations about substance use, recovery, mental health, and navigating high school culture.

Hosted by Jimmy Centeno and Saif Faghihy, we explore topics that many students deal with but don’t always talk about out loud. These are the stories behind the statistics—the unfiltered truth that you won’t always hear in a classroom or a flyer.

We also feature guest speakers, including recovering addicts and mental health advocates. These conversations are tough but necessary—and every single one is rooted in care and community.
