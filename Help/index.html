Help

If you’re here because you need help, or you’re trying to help someone else, you’re in the right place. This page is for students, friends, and families looking for support, answers, or just a place to start. You’ll find trusted resources for mental health, addiction recovery, crisis hotlines, school contacts, and community programs. Everything here is private, accessible, and meant to make getting help easier and less overwhelming. No one should have to deal with this alone, and you don’t have to. Whether you're struggling yourself or trying to support someone you care about, these resources are here for you. It takes strength to ask for help, and this page is a reminder that you’re not alone, and you’re not stuck.
