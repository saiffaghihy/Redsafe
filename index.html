Welcome to RedSafe

Welcome to RedSafe, a campaign by Redwood students for Redwood students.

RedSafe isn’t just a website, it’s a movement. Built by students who care, this initiative tackles a growing issue in our community, teen drug and alcohol abuse. In Marin County, substance use has become dangerously normalized, especially among high school students. It’s not just present, it’s accepted, and that needs to change.

We’re here to challenge the culture, not the people.

We believe change starts with awareness, honest conversation, education, and action.

On this site, you’ll find personal stories, researched solutions, a podcast series, mental health resources, and ways to get involved. Whether you’re a student, parent, or educator, we invite you to explore, reflect, and help make Redwood a safer place for everyone.

This is RedSafe, where recovery, awareness, and prevention begin.
